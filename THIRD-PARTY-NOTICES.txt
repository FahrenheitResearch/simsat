scripts now use find-msvc-tools 0.1.9 to locate the Windows SDK resource
compiler. That exact MIT OR Apache-2.0 crate was already locked and is already
listed under the MIT license below, so this change adds no crate, version, or
license text. `cargo tree --target x86_64-pc-windows-msvc -e normal -p
simsat_studio` confirms that it is not linked into the shipped executable;
`-e build` confirms the direct build-time edge.
